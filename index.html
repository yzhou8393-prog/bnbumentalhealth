Understanding Your Support

Why did I receive a message?

Messages are sent by human counselors who review wellness patterns. They are not automated. A real person reviewed your case before reaching out.

What data was used?

Only your check-in responses and consented data sources. Your grades, discipline records, and private messages are never accessed.

Is this confidential?

Yes. All wellness communications are FERPA-compliant and only accessible to assigned counseling staff. Nothing is shared with faculty or administration.

AI Limitations & Disclaimers

  • SCWIS AI is a decision-support tool, not a decision-maker. All final decisions are made by qualified human counselors.
  • AI may miss some students who need support (false negatives). Self-referral and peer referral remain essential.
  • Patterns reflect correlation, not causation. A student reducing library visits may have found a better study spot.
  • Students can opt out at any time. AI predictions are never used for academic evaluation or disciplinary action.
